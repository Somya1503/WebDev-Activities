Delicious Cupcake

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups (190g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ cup (115g) unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup (150g) granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • ½ cup (120ml) whole milk

Intructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a 12-cup muffin tin with liners.
  2. Whisk flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Cream butter and sugar until fluffy, then mix in eggs and vanilla.
  4. Add dry ingredients alternately with milk, mixing just until combined.
  5. Divide batter into liners and bake for 18–22 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Cool cupcakes in the pan for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack.
  7. Beat butter, gradually add powdered sugar, vanilla, salt, and cream until fluffy.
  8. Frost cooled cupcakes and decorate as desired.

Enjoy your homemade cupcakes
